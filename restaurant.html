A Taste of Italy in Fort Lauderdale

Vitolo brings authentic Italian cuisine to Fort Lauderdale Beach, offering a menu that celebrates Italy's rich culinary traditions with a modern touch. Our chef-driven approach focuses on quality ingredients and time-honored techniques.

From handmade pastas to wood-fired pizzas and fresh seafood, each dish tells a story of Italian heritage and passion for food. Our extensive wine list features selections from Italy's finest regions, perfectly paired to complement your meal.

Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, family celebration, or business meeting, we promise an unforgettable culinary journey through Italy's diverse regions in our elegant yet comfortable setting.
